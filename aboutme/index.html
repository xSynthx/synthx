About Me!

I am a 16 year old high school student in the United States! I started hacking when I was about 14 years old, it all started when someone told me about this thing called Tails. I asked them about it, and they started telling me, "its something called a live boot USB" and they were telling me how it worked and what it did, so I went home that night fascinated, I looked tails up, and how to make it live boot. I then started looking up other live boot USB's not knowing the correct terms for it (Operating Systems). My first one that I ever booted up was "Anonymous OS". I know right?!? You gotta start somewhere though. Then came along a church that my dad was gonna start running sound for. I was lonely there at first until one day a kid come up and sat next to me and said "is that terraria". Me and him are now really good friends. I started to learn more about Linux live USB's. Then came along this distro called Kali Linux. I extracted it to a flashdrive and booted it up. I found something called SQLMap. I was very curious about this thing called SQLMap, so I looked it up on google, found a beginner guide to it. I then found a website that was vulnerable to the injection. I dropped the entire database. This is all where my adventures about hacking started! I now know little SQLi, I know about holding sessions on people (reverse shell scripts), and currently learning more! I own a hacker forums called The Hacker Community! It isnt that popular yet, but I am trying my best to promote it. I also am a programmer. I am pretty good at HTML, and CSS. I am also currently learning python.
